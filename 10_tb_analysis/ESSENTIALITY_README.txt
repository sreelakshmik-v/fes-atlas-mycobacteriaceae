Mtb Fe-S essentiality: per-gene intersection with DeJesus et al. 2017 (mBio 8:e02133-16) Table S3.
Method: Mtb (H37Rv, GCF_000195955.2) Fe-S loci -> Rv-number (via UniProt) -> DeJesus HMM essentiality call.
Calls: ES=essential, ESD=essential-domain, GD=growth-defect, NE=non-essential, GA=growth-advantage.
Coverage: 57/72 Mtb Fe-S genes mapped to Rv-numbers and joined.
Result: 13 ES (22%), 16 ES+GD (28%) vs ~11% essential genome-wide => Fe-S proteome enriched for essential genes.
Essential families: IspG, IspH (MEP), SUF biogenesis, Aconitase, IlvD, ThiC, NadA, LipA, GltB, LeuC (drug-target relevant).
mtb_fes_dejesus.tsv = per-gene table (locus, gene, family, Rv, DeJesus_call).
